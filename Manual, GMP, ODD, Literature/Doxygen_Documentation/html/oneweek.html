Weekly processes

Each week the model will go through following processes:

  • reset weekly variables (orig. by F.May)
  • Set the weekly resources in cell (e.g. seasonal variation)
  • Calculates current ZOIs (orig. by F. May)
  • Sets current cover of grid and PFTs
  • Resource distribution between plants
  • Plant processes (e.g. growth) (orig. by F.May)
  • Disturbances (apart from herbicide)
  • Remove dead and trampled plants
  • Seed mortality
  • Seed and ramet establishment
  • Winter dieback
  • Output of PFT and grid variables

The function CGridEnvir::OneWeek() coordinates sequence and occurence of weekly processes on the grid.
