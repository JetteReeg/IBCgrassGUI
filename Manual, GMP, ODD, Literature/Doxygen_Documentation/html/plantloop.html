Plant processes

The plant will go through the following processes each week

  • Growth (orig. by F.May)
  • Dispersal of ramets (orig. by I. Steinhauer)
  • Growth of spacer (orig. by I. Steinhauer)
  • Seed dispersal (orig. by F.May)
  • Plant mortality (orig. by F.May)

The function CGrid::PlantLoop() coordinates sequence and occurence of processes.
